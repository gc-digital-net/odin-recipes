Lasagna Recipe

How to Make Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is made with layers of pasta, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and marinara sauce. It is typically baked in the oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain.
  3. Then, add marinara sauce to a layer in the bottom of a baking dish.
  4. Next, add a layer of the prepared lasagna noodles.
  5. Then, add a layer of ricotta cheese.
  6. Next, add another layer of noodles.
  7. Then, add mozzarella cheese.
  8. Finally, add a layer of meat sauce.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil, and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
