Scrambled Eggs Recipe

How to Make Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled Eggs is a classic breakfast dish that is made with eggs, butter, and salt. It is typically cooked in a pan until the eggs are scrambled and fluffy.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, melt butter.
  2. Then, add eggs to the pan and scramble.
  3. Next, season with salt.
  4. Finally, serve hot.
